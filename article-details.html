Who is at the helm of this coaching firm?

The coaching firm is under the distinguished leadership of Mr. Suhel Taj.

How long is the coaching program for?

The coaching program is designed to run for a minimum duration of one year.

What is the expected timeframe to witness improvements in my child?

Typically, noticeable improvements can be observed within 2-3 months. However, for more comprehensive and enduring results, we recommend enrolling your child for a duration of at least one year.

Can I enroll my child for more than one year?

Absolutely. In fact, extending your child's enrollment will provide an extended period for us to foster their growth. The longer your child is nurtured, the greater the potential for enhanced development.

If parents already nurture their children, why is 'Nurture My Child' necessary?

While parental nurturing is essential and irreplaceable, 'Nurture My Child' offers a unique expertise in nurturing, personality development, and career growth. Our specialized curriculum imparts skills that are honed by experts, creating an environment of proficiency that empowers your child's development holistically.

Can the timings for the lectures be adjusted to fit my child's schedule?

Absolutely, we understand the need for flexible lecture timings to cater to various schedules. We're committed to accommodating your availability while ensuring productive learning. However, please note that our sessions will not be scheduled between 10:00 PM and 4:00 AM.

How do you access the progress of students, and how are parents kept informed about their child's development?

Regular assessments, evaluations, and progress reports are integral parts of our coaching program. We maintain open communication with parents, sharing insights into their child's achievements and areas for improvement. This collaborative approach ensures transparency and active involvement in your child's educational journey.

We understand the significance of fostering your child's growth and potential. Our coaching program, directed by Mr. Suhel Taj, is meticulously designed to provide lasting improvements. Within a span of just a few months, you'll begin to witness noticeable progress, but for truly transformative outcomes, consider enrolling your child for an extended period. This way, you'll be giving your child the opportunity to thrive within an environment of expertise, and our 'Nurture My Child' program will seamlessly complement the nurturing efforts you invest in as parents.

How to book a consultation?

To schedule a consultation, please reach out to us using any of the contact links provided in the "Contact Us" section on our website. Once you get in touch, our dedicated team will guide you through the consultation booking process. We believe in providing a personalized experience, ensuring that you receive the necessary information and guidance tailored to your specific inquiries and preferences. This consultation serves as an invaluable opportunity for you to explore our coaching approach, curriculum details, and address any queries you may have while charting the best path forward for your child's educational journey.
