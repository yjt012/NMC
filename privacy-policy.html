1. Information We Collect

NurtureMyChild collects data for the following general purposes and for other specific ones that are important:

  1. User Information: When users create accounts or interact with our platform, we collect personal information such as names, ages, contact details, and in some cases, school information. For users under 13, we obtain parental consent before collecting any personal information.
  2. Counseling Interaction Data: During counseling sessions, users may share personal experiences and thoughts. This data is treated with the highest level of confidentiality.

2. How We Use Information

  1. Counseling Services: User information and counseling interaction data are used to provide personalized counseling services. Our professional counselors rely on this information to offer relevant guidance and support.
  2. Counseling Interaction Data: During counseling sessions, users may share personal experiences and thoughts. This data is treated with the highest level of confidentiality.

3. Information Sharing

  1. Confidentiality: Counseling interaction data is strictly confidential. However, if there is a concern for the user's safety or the safety of others, we may need to involve appropriate parties.
  2. Parental Access: For users under 13, we provide parents or guardians with the ability to review their child's interactions with our platform.

4. Data Security:

  1. Secure Transmission: We use encryption to secure data transmission over the internet.
  2. Account Security: Users are responsible for maintaining the security of their accounts and are encouraged not to share login credentials.

5. Parental Consent and Control:

  1. Parental Involvement: We encourage parental involvement in children's online activities. Parents can review, modify, or delete their child's information.
  2. Revoking Consent: Parents can revoke their consent for their child's participation and request the deletion of their child's information.

6. Compliance

  1. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA): We adhere to the requirements of COPPA, ensuring the safety and privacy of children online.
  2. Terms of Use: Our Privacy Policy is aligned with our Terms and Conditions. By using our platform, users agree to both documents.

7. Updates

  1. Policy Changes: We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy. Users will be notified of any significant changes.

8. Contact Us:

  1. By using NurtureMyChild.com's online counseling services, you acknowledge and agree to this Privacy Policy. Your trust is important to us, and we are committed to maintaining a secure and nurturing environment for children and their families.

9. Data Retention:

  1. Counseling Data: We retain counseling interaction data for a limited period, after which it is securely deleted.
  2. Parental Control: Parents have the option to request deletion of their child's information.
